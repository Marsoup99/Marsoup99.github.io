Road Rage Rampage

Personal project.

A classic 3 lane runner game combine with roguelike gameplay.

Gameplay demo.

Some main features of the game:

1. Collisions:

Based on the collision position determined by the box colliders of two cars, different responses will be generated. When the cars collide, the collision position will be calculated based on their box colliders, enabling identification of whether the impact occurred at the front or back, left or right. The appropriate response will then be initiated based on this information.

2. Enemies:

Using raycast to check surrounding area of the enemy for potential obstacles and dodge obstacles if possible.

Enemies behavior driven by simple state machine base on the distance between them and player. Then I can divine enemies to 3 type and using Scriptable Object for easy management.

Every enemy is equipped with a different weapon, while bosses and mini-bosses boast two weapons along with their own set of unique skills.

3. Items:

24 items including weapons, armor, nitro engines, and relics to enhance gameplay strategy.

Certain items within the game provide increase player stat (hp, nitro speed,..) or buffs to player damage, such as granting increased damage against burning enemies. Additionally, some items may possess their own unique effects, offering further enhancements or abilities to the player.

4. Elements:

In the game, there are four types of elemental damage available: fire, toxic, electric, and normal. Each element exhibits unique interactions. For instance, fire damage inflicts increased damage to enemies with red health but less damage to armor or shield and has a chance to apply a burn effect.
